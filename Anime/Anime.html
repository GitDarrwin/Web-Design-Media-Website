Most Famous Animes of 2022

Editors' choice

Attack on Titan

After his hometown is destroyed and his mother is killed,
young Eren Jaeger vows to cleanse the earth of the Titans
that have brought humanity to the brink of extinction.


Demon Slayer

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive,
Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly.
Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family
and cure his sister, and turn her as human again.

Jujutsu Kaisen

A boy swallows a cursed talisman - the finger of a demon,
and becomes cursed himself. He enters a shaman's school to be
able to locate the demon's other body parts and exorcise himself.
In that progress he encounters foes he didn't even dreamof.

Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, a mischievous adolescent ninja, as he searches
for recognition and dreams of becoming the Hokage, village's leader
and strongest ninja Naruto Uzumaki, is a loud, hyperactive ninja
who searches for approval and recognition, as well as to become
Hokage, who is acknowledged as the leader of all ninja in the village.
